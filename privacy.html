Privacy Policy

ToolsMatic is designed to be privacy-first. This policy explains what data we handle and how we keep it minimal.

What we collect

  • Most tools run entirely in your browser; your inputs stay on your device.
  • We may collect basic, non-personal analytics (page views, performance) to keep the site stable.
  • No account, login, or upload is required for the tools listed on this site.

Cookies and storage

Some tools use localStorage in your browser to remember preferences (for example, saved favorites). We do not use these values for tracking.

Third-party services

The site may use lightweight analytics or ad services that collect aggregated usage data. These services should not receive the text or files you process inside the tools.

Data you provide

Do not paste sensitive personal, financial, or secret information into the tools if you are unsure about your device or network security. You are responsible for the content you process.

Security

We aim to maintain a secure site, but no system is perfectly secure. Use the tools at your own discretion.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
