Frequently Asked Questions

How many quotes does the generator include?

We have 525+ quotes across 15 categories, with 35 quotes per category. Each quote is hand-selected for wisdom, relevance, and impact.

Can I use these quotes commercially?

Most quotes are either by unknown authors or are in the public domain. For attributed quotes, we recommend doing your own due diligence if using commercially. For personal use, social media, presentations, and educational purposes, you're generally finebut always credit the author when known.

Do my favorite quotes sync across devices?

Favorites are saved in your browser's local storage, so they stay on the device where you saved them. They won't sync to other devices, but this also means your data never leaves your computerit's completely private.

Can I suggest quotes to add?

We love hearing from users! While we don't have a formal submission system yet, we regularly update our quote collection based on user feedback. Check back often for new additions.

Is there a limit to how many quotes I can favorite?

Nope! Save as many as you want. Your browser's storage can easily handle hundreds of quotes, so feel free to build your personal wisdom library.

Why don't I see author names for all quotes?

Many of our quotes are modern wisdom from the internet, industry best practices, or collected wisdom where the original author is unknown. We credit authors whenever possible, but sometimes great ideas are truly collaborative or anonymous.

Can I export my favorite quotes?

Currently, favorites are view-only in the app, but you can easily copy them individually using the Copy button. We're considering adding bulk export features in future updates.

Does this work offline?

Yes! Once the page loads, everything runs in your browser. No internet connection needed to generate quotes. (The Tweet button will require internet, obviously!)

Are there keyboard shortcuts?

Not yet, but that's a great idea for a future update! For now, everything is accessible with simple clicks.
